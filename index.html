Companies and teams use many online services such as Dropbox, Google Apps, Basecamp, etc. Since these online services are essentially isolated islands with their own interface, access control, etc., it is hard to work with all these services. cloudHQ integrates all your online services and your team into social-network-like interface with unified search, sharing, and team management which can be accessed directly from your Google Apps cloudHQ - Simplify Teamwork in the Cloud
